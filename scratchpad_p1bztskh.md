### Vision:
Empowering adaptation to the rapidly changing world, and to engineer the changes to be good. Every learner at OLIVEMOUNT is moulded as 'GLOBAL CITIZEN' with our 5 C's approach.

### Mission:
- To provide a learning environment which is safe, supportive and characterized by high expectations.
- To produce 'Citizens of the world' who are knowledgeable, compassionate and contributing members of the society.
- To foster child's natural curiosity and love of learning.
- To provide a broad and balanced curriculum that enables every child to fulfill their potential.
- To develop a sense of respect, responsibility and resilience in every child.

### 5 C's Approach:
1. **COMMUNICATION:** Clarity in Expression and Thought
2. **CONFIDENCE:** See the "I'M POSSIBLE" in "IMPOSSIBLE"
3. **CREATIVITY:** Igniting IMAGINATION, INNOVATION, AESTHETIC SENSE
4. **COMPETENCY:** To Reason, Logical and Critical Thinking, Concept Application
5. **CHARACTER:** To demonstrate Trustworthiness, Respect, Responsibility, Fairness, Care and Citizenship

### Academics - Curriculum Stages (CBSE):
- **Foundational Stage (Pre-KG to Class II):** Focus on play-based learning, basic literacy, and numeracy.
- **Preparatory Stage (Classes III to V):** Introduction to formal subjects, interactive learning.
- **Middle Stage (Classes VI to VIII):** Deeper understanding of concepts, critical thinking, and project-based learning.
- **Secondary Stage (Classes IX to XII):** Focus on advanced academics, career guidance, and board examinations.
